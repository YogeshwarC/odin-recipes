Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

  1. Othapam batter
  2. 2 carrots
  3. 16 spoons of ghee
  4. Idli podi

First clean the Tawa using water and the regular broom used to clean it. Take a bowl of othapam batter and make a moderate sized round with it in the Tawa. Add finely chopped carrots to the Othapam Add 6 spoons of ghee inside the Othapam and 10 spoons of ghee around the Othapam Take Idli podi and sprinkle it over the Othapam like drizzling rain.
